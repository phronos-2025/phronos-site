FROM THE CONSTITUTION
AXIOM I
The Observatory

Phronos is an observatory—a place of structured observation rather than instruction. Our instruments generate telemetry—structured observations of cognition in action—and our analyses translate that telemetry into findings you can examine for yourself.

AXIOM II
The Question of This Era

The defining question of this era is not who am I? but where am I still myself? Our instruments reveal the dimensions where human reasoning remains distinctively human.

AXIOM V
Sovereignty and the Commons

Your data belongs to you. Consent is explicit and revocable. For those who wish to engage without leaving any trace, local mode exists: processing happens on your device, nothing is transmitted, no account is required.

AXIOM VII
The Limits of Our Authority

Phronos will never claim to know what is best for you. We build mirrors that also take notes. What you see in them—and what you do next—remains yours.
